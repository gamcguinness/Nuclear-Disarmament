Effects of Nuclear Armament

The effect of Nuclear Armament is the possibility of nuclear war. This nuclear war could happen by: military conflict involving an armed nation; accidental nuclear war caused by a technical or communication breakdown; nuclear terrorism perpetrated by an individual organisation with access to these weapons. Regardless of which scenario takes place, the situation is likely to escalate with retaliation by the affected nation(s). This would lead to the following devastations:

Climate
A nuclear war would emit harmful chemicals that accelerate climate change and destroy the ozone layer
Health
A nuclear war would immediately kill and injure hundreds of millions of people as well as cause health problems from the radiation.
Environment
A nuclear war would
cause massive amounts
of radioactivity destroying ecosystems and living creatures.
Economy
A nuclear war would destroy the global economy with the destruction of infrastructure, disruption of supply chains and uncertainty.
Humanitarian
A nuclear war would
cause the largest humanitarian crisis with the massive displacement of people and the contamination of food
and water.
